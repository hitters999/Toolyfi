Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. Learn how we protect your data and what information we collect.

Introduction

Welcome to Toolyfi ("we," "us," "our," or "Company"). Toolyfi is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website.

✓ We respect your privacy and never sell your data to third parties

Information We Collect

1. Information You Provide Directly

  • Contact Information: When you contact us, we collect your name, email address, and message content
  • Feedback: Any feedback, suggestions, or feature requests you provide
  • User-Generated Content: Files or data you upload to use our tools (processed locally)

2. Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent on pages, clicks, scroll depth
  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, device type
  • Location Data: General location based on IP address (country level)
  • Analytics: How you interact with our tools and features

3. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for basic website functionality
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand user behavior (Google Analytics)
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your settings and preferences

How We Use Your Information

We use collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our services
  • To improve and optimize our tools and website
  • To respond to your inquiries and support requests
  • To send you updates and announcements (with your consent)
  • To analyze usage patterns and user behavior
  • To detect and prevent fraud or abuse
  • To comply with legal obligations

Data Storage & Security

Your data security is our priority:

Local Processing

Most of your data (files, inputs) is processed locally in your browser. We don't store or transmit this data to our servers unless you explicitly choose to.

Transmission Security

  • All data transmitted over HTTPS (encrypted)
  • SSL certificates protect data in transit
  • Regular security audits and updates

Server Security

  • Industry-standard security measures
  • Limited access to personal data
  • Regular backups and disaster recovery

✓ We use GitHub Pages for hosting - one of the most secure platforms available

Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

Access

You have the right to access personal information we hold about you

Correction

You can request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information

Deletion

You can request deletion of your personal data (right to be forgotten)

Opt-Out

You can opt-out of marketing communications and analytics tracking

Data Portability

You can request your data in a portable format

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

Google Analytics

Helps us understand user behavior and website performance. Google Privacy Policy

Google Search Console

Monitors website search performance. Data processed according to Google's Privacy Policy

Hosting (GitHub Pages)

Website hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub Privacy Policy

Note: We do not share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

Children's Privacy

Toolyfi is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will take steps to delete such information and terminate the child's account.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the bottom of this page. Your continued use of Toolyfi indicates your acceptance of these changes.
